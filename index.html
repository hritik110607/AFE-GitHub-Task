01 / ABOUT

Who I Am

I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Information Technology at REVA University, and I genuinely enjoy the process of learning how things work — and then making them work better. Whether it's untangling a stubborn bug, designing a cleaner algorithm, or wiring up a robot that actually follows the line it's supposed to, I find real satisfaction in solving problems end to end.

Outside of coursework, I build small projects to test what I've learned: embedded systems with Arduino, scripts in Python and C, and the occasional web page just to see an idea come to life on screen. I'm early in my journey, but deliberate about it — every project is a chance to pick up a new tool, language or way of thinking.
